The business challenge

Finance teams still spend too much time preparing transactions.

Invoices and supporting documents arrive through multiple channels, data is re-keyed, exceptions are discovered late and evidence is rebuilt during close or audit.

01

Documents require manual entry

Finance reads invoices, receipts and statements and transfers the same information into several systems.

02

Exceptions lack clear ownership

Missing orders, receipts, coding, tax data or approvals create email loops and delayed posting.

03

Control evidence is assembled afterwards

Teams reconstruct validations, decisions and supporting documents during close, audit or dispute resolution.

Move finance administration to an exception-based model.

SELYVO captures, validates and routes transactions inside governed workflows, while people focus on material decisions and exceptions.

AI-assisted captureExtract supplier, invoice, tax, line, amount and payment data from email, upload, scan or API.
Controlled validationCheck duplicates, suppliers, bank accounts, tax, coding, required evidence and policy.
Matching and workflowMatch orders, receipts and invoices; route only unresolved differences to named owners.

How SELYVO solves it

A controlled flow from document to posting.

Routine work is automated; exceptions remain visible, owned and auditable.

STEP 01

Capture documents

Receive invoices, receipts, statements and supporting evidence through email, scan, upload or API.

STEP 02

Extract and validate

Recognise data and check completeness, supplier, tax, bank, duplicate and coding rules.

STEP 03

Match transactions

Compare invoices with orders, receipts, contracts, cards or approved expense data.

STEP 04

Resolve exceptions

Assign missing information, mismatches and approvals to the correct owner with full context.

STEP 05

Approve and post

Route material decisions and send approved data to ERP, accounting or payment processing.

STEP 06

Measure and improve

Track touchless rate, cycle time, exception causes, ageing and data quality.

Daily operation
